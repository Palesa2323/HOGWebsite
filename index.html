About Us

House of Galotolo is a specialist tutoring centre in Kuruman, Northern Cape, that focusses on assisting learners and students in achieving academic success. We provide specialised supplementary sessions in technical disciplines, with the goal of providing young minds with the necessary information and practical skills for academic and life success. Our commitment to education creates a friendly environment in which students can develop, learn, and thrive. Join us on our journey to academic achievement!
